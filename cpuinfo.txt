Only bits available in all families are chosen.

UNDEF = Not defined in the manual. Don't rely on the CPU having the data if UNDEF.

If the bits are different than 10h, a leading * is added.

Since a decent amount of cpus have MaxVid/MinVid, use those for those cpus,
use hardcoded values for cpus without, it's possible the cpus without the info
have them, but AMD forgot to add them in the PDF's, but without CPU's to test
this theory it's better to use hardcoded values.

I won't support 14h because of the did/fid differences.

MaxCpuCof and MainPllOpEtc are similar.

The only real difference is the lack of MaxVid/MinVid on some CPUs and 12h have the CPU DID/FID in different bits.
